Who we are

Jim Musgrave

Jim Musgrave is the owner of English Majors Editors and Publishers, LLC in San Diego dedicated to providing quality enhanced eBooks and apps. He is also popular indie author and was a college professor and Supervisor of Management Development at Caltech in Pasadena. He is married to Ellen who was also a college professor.

Janet Bernice

Janet Bernice is an author, faculty member for two online universities, and student mentor. She has an M.A degree in English and Writing from Weber State University and has been an executive book editor for two different publishers. Janet did technical writing, editing and writing of web page content for several corporations including Hewlett Packard and The Veterans Administration. She currently writes alternative scenarios for the Forevermore Choose Your Mystery series and serves as writer/editor for online books and content for English Majors LLC.

Tom Groentjes

Tom Groentjes has worked on sophisticated apps like the app that allows Dutch voters to connect with their party of interest, and an app that whisky drinkers use to find their favorite labels from all over the world. He prides himself on being able to manage people and "think outside the box." He is working on a Master's in Computer Science at Leiden University.

Bas van Stein

Bas van Stein loves to work at developing new software, and he can help you make your imagination come alive if you have a new app or game to develop. He also worked on the Embellisher app at EMRE, and we're happy to have him on board to assist you as well. Ari Bernabei

Ari Bernabei is our book illustrator and cover illustration creator. He can also do comics, as his series “Ekeing and Pimpin’” is a popular attraction online. He worked with owner Jim Musgrave to create the covers for his volume of short fiction, The Mayan Magician and Other Stories and Musgrave’s Disappearance at Mount Sinai (second mystery in the popular Pat O’Malley series) as well as the magnificent illustrations inside Steam City Pirates (fourth mystery in the series). What makes him a wonderful illustrator is his ability to translate into pictures exactly what your words portray on the page. Ari has his MFA degree in Fine Art from the prestigious San Francisco Art Institute. He would love to make your books stand out from the crowd and assist you in establishing your brand with a designed logo or other memorable image.
